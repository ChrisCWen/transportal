Description:

The image shows a simplified cell outline with marked basolateral side facing the brain interstitial space and apical/luminal side facing the blood. OCTN1, OCTN2, and OAT3 are placed as influx transporters on the basolateral side. OATP1A2 and OATP2B1 are apical influx transporters; while MDR1, BCRP, MRP1, MRP2, MRP4, and MRP5 are apical efflux transporters.
